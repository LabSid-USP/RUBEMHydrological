RUBEM Hydrological

Version 1.12.2-beta.2

Release notes · Licenses information

RUBEM Hydrological uses Rainfall rUnoff Balance Enhanced Model (RUBEM) to calculate monthly water balance (runoff, aquifer recharge, canopy interception and Base flow) with capabilities to consider changes in LULC over the time.
